Bangladesh 2.0

Idea, innovation, vision and challenges in New Bangladesh

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving technological advancements to make Bangladesh a leader in digital transformation.

Sustainability

Ensuring long-term environmental and economic stability through green initiatives and responsible governance.

Infrastructure

Building world-class infrastructure to enhance connectivity, trade, and economic opportunities.

Empowerment

Fostering an inclusive society by providing equal opportunities in education, business, and leadership.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh is on the path to transformation, driven by technology, sustainability, and the determination of its people. We must continue to embrace innovation for a brighter future. If we can imagine something, there is a good chance that it will happen. If we don't imagine it, there is almost no chance of it happening. (From A World of Three Zeros: The New Economics of Zero Poverty, Zero Unemployment, and Zero Net Carbon Emissions.)"

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Cheif Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
